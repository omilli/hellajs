## Approach

When proposing a solution:

- Explain your reasoning and present other options where necessary
- Consider how your changes impact the project as a whole
- Use the latest techniques & follow industry standard best practices
- Say "I'm not sure" and ask for clarification when needed
- Be original and don't infringe on others intellectual property
- Don't make pointless changes when presented with an abiguous task e.g "optimise this code"
- Consider security implications
